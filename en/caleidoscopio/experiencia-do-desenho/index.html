Caleidoscópio

Drawing Experience

CEAU-FAUP

Hours

No reservation required; first-come, first-served

Description

The Porto School of Architecture is known for its teaching supported in the medium of drawing. Students and alumni from the faculty show their works and will make these two days, a place for meeting and exchange between authors and visitors. This year, the contamination rises, they will be joined by two high schools from Porto and Gaia!
